10 HOBBIES FOR SENIOR CITIZENS

Everyone wants to stay fit, healthy and happy in their life. As years pass by life takes a toll on all of us. Someday or the other we become old. To stay active they need to pursue some hobbies which would bring joy and inner satisfaction. So here I am going to list some hobbies which the senior citizens can pursue.



Hobbies that come in the list


GARDENING

Gardening is the most common and stimulating hobby a senior citizen must pursue. Gardening not only provides mental peace and relieves stress but it also provides an ample amount of knowledge about wildlife and nature. It is an interesting hobby.

SOCIAL ACTIVITY

Engaging in social activity can be a good conscious hobby. If you are ready to help your environment or society by your services than it is warmly welcomed by all. A person can involve in some NGO and work for the welfare of society. A few tasks include in social activity are free health camps, planting trees, cleaning the environment and so on.

READING

There are no other hobbies where one can find enlightenment as we find in reading books. Just hook up with a book that interests you and there you are spending hours reading the book. This hobby is also very popular among all the age groups.

JOGGING OR EXERCISING

After retiring from work everyone would want to stay fit and stay away from diseases. For this, they need to exercise or join a yoga and fitness classes. This reduces stress and keeps diseases away. This is a really good pastime in which they can spend time.

VISITING RELATIVES

Visiting relatives and friends can be a good pastime for senior citizens. This would help them to interact and maintain healthy relations with relatives and friends. This would bring happiness and live life to the fullest.

TRAVELING

Traveling to places is a very good option for a retired person. This makes them explore different unknown places on Earth. They would come to know about various places, lifestyles, people, natural vegetation, and climate and so on.

TAKING CARE OF PETS

Adopting pets and taking care of them can be a great pastime for the retired person. They can take their pets for a walk, playing with them, bathing them, feeding them. These pets would also give them an extra companion to lonely ones.

PAINTING

Who does not like creating masterpieces? Sitting aloof with a brush and canvas can be a heaven for art lovers. This is a giant time killer and also makes use of time for making something useful, beautiful and worth displaying. Create some of these and hung those over the wall to showcase your lost talent.

WRITING

A pen is mightier than a sword. Yes, this can be the motivational line which can inspire retired persons to take upon writing if they have some passion for writing. Writing can range from articles to writing poems and stories. They can even write about their whole life in a diary. This is also a good hobby.

GAMING

Last but not least gaming is also a good hobby for elderly persons. These range from playing cards or playing a sports game or an action game on console. All are great as gaming has been very popular among all generations these days.

So these were some of the top hobbies which the elderly person can adopt after their retirement from work.
